Savitha (Savi) - Biography
Leadership Philosophy

To Savitha (Savi), "Breaking the PACE" means breaking free from expectations to set her rhythm, challenging norms, and taking proactive steps to drive positive change in her community. As a people leader, Savitha thrives in an environment where she can push boundaries, including being on the Women in Technology panel, Stem Next, LAA, and other influential platforms.

Educational Background

Raised in India, Savi brings a rich educational background with a Master's Degree in Science (India), Computer Network Technology (California), and an MBA in Executive Management (JWMI Washington D.C.). Her diverse academic foundation reflects her commitment to continuous learning and excellence.

Recognition & Awards

In October 2024, Savitha received a prestigious award from YWCA Bucks County PA in their 33rd Salute to Women who make a difference, specifically in the "Racial and Social Justice" category, recognizing her significant contributions to creating positive social change.

Dan - Biography
Professional Experience

Dan has worked in technology for over 45 years, with a focus on AI and machine learning for the past decade. He currently applies LLMs and advanced data science tools in a production setting, helping his organization explore the growing potential of artificial intelligence. His daily work centers around concept development, prompt engineering, and Python-based experimentation.

Journey & Philosophy

A lifelong learner, Dan is proud of his ability to keep pace with a fast-changing field — whether it's implementing new AI techniques at work or experimenting with cutting-edge models at home. His technical journey began in high school in 1974, where he first encountered computers and instantly saw their sci-fi promise. That early spark led him through roles in academia and telecommunications, always with a strong self-driven approach to learning and discovery.

Community Impact

Beyond his professional achievements, Dan informally mentors others and occasionally leads data science workshops. His passion lies in solving problems independently and imagining the future of AI — including the emergence of artificial superintelligence as a force for good.

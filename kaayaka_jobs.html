About Kaayaka Jobs

Kaayaka Jobs is an initiative of Basava Philosophy Research Foundation, California registered non-profit organization. This platform serves as a comprehensive forum for both job seekers and employers in the technology sector.

Our community is designed for those who seek jobs and also for those who would like to hire for their companies or refer others. It operates as a self-help and mutual support group, fostering connections between talented professionals and growing companies.

Mission: To bridge the gap between talented tech professionals and innovative companies through community-driven networking and mutual support.

Kaayaka Community

Hi everyone! This community is an offshoot of Basava Philosophy Research Foundation, registered non-profit organization of California state. This is a managed group designed to help job seekers and professionals connect with hiring tech workers, employers, and industry professionals.

Currently we are focusing on IT jobs and job seekers can ask for referrals while professionals can post requirements if they are hiring from their companies. Our platform facilitates meaningful connections that benefit both job seekers and employers.

USA Focus: This group is specifically designed for USA residents and focuses on opportunities within the United States job market.

About Kaayaka AI Mantapa

Kaayaka AI Mantapa is a part of Kaayaka Community, an initiative of Basava Philosophy Research Foundation, California, CA registered non-profit organization. Kaayaka AI Mantapa is a self-help, mutual support learning group designed to foster collaborative learning in the field of Artificial Intelligence.

This is a forum where people can exchange learning materials and freely ask AI-related questions. Other members can answer and AI moderators are here just to manage smooth communications in the group. Our community is built on the principles of mutual respect, knowledge sharing, and collective growth.

Mission: To create a supportive environment where AI enthusiasts can learn, share, and grow together through collaborative knowledge exchange.

Community Guidelines

Please Follow These Guidelines

Exercise reasonableness and common sense in posting questions or answers
This is not a place for advertisements, arguments, or self-promoting materials
Share materials that genuinely help others learn and grow
If you need help, you can DM the member who posted relevant content
Exercise decency & respect privacy and value other members' time
Think before you post - consider language, ethics, and timing
Stay away from posting illegal or unethical requests
Maintain professional and respectful communication at all times

Open to All: This group is non-racial, non-language specific, non-geographical region specific and welcomes all people across USA who are passionate to learn AI.

Important Notice & Disclaimer

Eligibility: This group is only for USA residents, even though at times group admins may take offshore virtual associates to help manage the group.

Liability: BPRF, moderators, or any members are not responsible for any damage that is caused by any discussion, material, posts, or privacy of your data. Participate at your own discretion and responsibility.

Data Privacy: Please be mindful of sharing personal or sensitive information. Protect your privacy and respect others' privacy as well.

Community Safety: We maintain a safe, inclusive learning environment. Report any violations of community guidelines to moderators.

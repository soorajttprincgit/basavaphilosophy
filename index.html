We Promote Basava Philosophy Principles for Social Prosperity

The Basava Philosophy Foundation is a United States nonprofit organization originated in California committed to fostering social prosperity through the timeless principles of 12th Century South Indian Philosopher, Social Reformer Basavanna. We are guided by the vision of applying his Principles for societal upliftment.

How We Create Change

Scientific Research & Education: Focused on applying Basava's principles to social prosperity by promoting scientific R&D projects on job creation (Kayaka) and food security (Dasoha), alongside establishing educational mantapas to spread knowledge and opportunity.

Social Community Building: Aimed at popularizing Basava's teachings, the Foundation nurtures vibrant communities such as Basava Balaga, creating platforms for dialogue, collaboration, and cultural promotion.

Change Catalysts for Society: Working to reiterate the values of equality, caste-free and race-free society, and women's empowerment through dedicated forums and mantapas.

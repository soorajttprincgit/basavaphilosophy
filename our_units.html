Our Specialized Research Units

Educational Research Unit

Develops innovative educational programs based on Basava's principles, focusing on inclusive learning methodologies and bias-free curriculum development.

Social Science Research Unit

Conducts scientific research on social bias reduction, equality promotion, and the practical application of Basava's philosophy in contemporary society.

Community Engagement Unit

Builds and nurtures Basava Balaga communities, organizing events, workshops, and cultural programs to spread awareness of Basava's teachings.

Kayaka (Work) Development Unit

Focuses on creating sustainable employment opportunities and skill development programs based on Basava's concept of meaningful work (Kayaka).

Dasoha (Service) Unit

Implements Basava's principle of Dasoha through food security programs, knowledge sharing initiatives, and community service projects.

Women Empowerment Unit

Dedicated to advancing women's rights and empowerment through education, leadership development, and advocacy programs.
